* Open the projector and remove the led board and the controller. Keep only the genuine power board with the fan.
* Take the old power supply connection (+- 4.5V) and soldered it to directy to the RGBFight board (Pads from unpopulated C5 suits perfectly)
* Prepare the adaptor plate on an laser cutter or CNC. Reduce the the side lips until they into the mounting brackets.
* Screw the matrix panel with attached RGBFighter to the adaptor. Take care about the center alignmentm, adjustable slot holes
